About

This is a simple web app that utilizes IOT technology.BMS systems are “Intelligent” microprocessor based controller networks installed to monitor and control a building's technical systems and services ,microprocessor-based such as air conditioning, ventilation, lighting and hydraulics.The web app enables remote monitoring and control of the integrated building equipment. This enables remote notifications on faults and provides real-time data on equipment performance and energy consumption. It also allows scheduling and remote control of equipment hence increasing efficiency.

Features

  • Lighting control and monitoring
  • Energy consumption monitoring
  • Access control management
  • HVAC systems control and monitoring
  • Fire systems management
  • Tenancy management
